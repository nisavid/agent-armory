# Forge Tour

Status: Forge Seed

The Forge Tour is the human-facing introduction to the Agent Equipment Forge.
It explains what the Forge is for and points readers toward the durable Forge
Canon without exposing the agent-only operating machinery first.

## What The Forge Is

The Agent Equipment Forge is the Armory's method and supporting artifacts for
designing, building, validating, and maintaining Agent Equipment. It helps a
Smith decide what equipment is needed, where it belongs, what evidence supports
it, and how it should be validated before use.

The Forge Seed establishes the first working shape: vocabulary, runbooks,
evidence rules, harness capability records, templates, examples, downstream
Blueprints, validation, and closeout discipline.
